## Overview

The Respira for WordPress WooCommerce Add-on extends Respira's safety-first workflow to your WooCommerce store. 54 tools that connect commerce data to page builder visuals, with a snapshot, a dry-run preview, and an approval step on every change.

**Pricing:**
- **Studio and Founder plans:** included free, no separate purchase
- **Maker and Builder plans:** €95/year as a separate add-on

No free trial on the WooCommerce add-on. The core Respira plugin has a 7-day trial, but the WooCommerce add-on is paid from day one.

## What's included (54 tools)

The add-on grew from commerce CRUD into the full storefront layer. The areas:

**Products, categories, and brands**
- Create, read, update, and duplicate simple and variable products
- Full CRUD for product categories, tags, and brands

**Variations, attributes, and swatches**
- Variation CRUD and one-call variation-matrix generation with a dry-run preview
- Global attributes, plus the WooCommerce 10.9 colour and image swatches (wc-visual)

**Product configurator (Staggs)**
- Drive the Staggs product configurator by prompt: enable it per product, read its setup, and toggle options per dealer. Allow-listed and snapshotted. The piece no other WooCommerce MCP has.

**Orders, refunds, and notes**
- Order listing, status updates (which trigger WooCommerce emails), and order notes
- Refunds are dry-run by default: money only moves when you explicitly confirm, and a refund is capped to the order's remaining refundable total

**Coupons and customers**
- Full coupon CRUD, and customer CRUD. Customer writes are PII-aware: confirm-to-delete, and IDs only in the audit log

**Inventory and AI-readiness**
- Stock levels, stock status, and low-stock detection
- An AI-readiness score (0-100) per product and across the catalog (`product_ai_readiness`, `catalog_ai_readiness`) so an agent knows what to fix before it sells

**Storefront design intelligence**
- Read your shop and product pages in the active builder, score them, and edit product cards and checkout layout through the builder abstraction. Supported on Elementor, Divi, Bricks, Flatsome, and Gutenberg; other builders return a clear unsupported message

**Catalog operations and pricing**
- Advanced product filtering, bulk product and price updates (percentage, absolute, or set-value) with a 100-product cap and snapshots, catalog-health audits, natural-language product search, and snapshot-backed pricing rollback

## Safety

Every WooCommerce write operation creates a before/after snapshot. Every bulk operation has dry_run mode. 100-product hard cap per call prevents runaway AI mistakes. PII is redacted by default on order data.

- **Snapshots:** every product write (create, update, stock change) captures before and after states
- **Dry-run:** bulk_update_prices, bulk_update_products, bulk_update_stock, schedule_sale all support `dry_run: true`
- **Refunds dry-run by default:** a refund is previewed and capped to the order's remaining refundable total; money moves only on explicit confirmation
- **Hard cap:** maximum 100 products per bulk call
- **Audit trail:** all commerce writes logged to the Respira audit log
- **PII redaction:** customer email, phone, and address redacted by default on order endpoints

<Callout kind="tip">

The financial core of your store stays under WooCommerce and your payment gateway. Respira stages and snapshots changes. You confirm important updates yourself.

</Callout>

## Prompt examples

```text
Run analyze_shop_page on my WooCommerce shop page.
Tell me what's wrong with the product card hierarchy
and what I should fix first.
```

```text
Find all products in "Summer Collection" and increase
```

```text
Run analyze_catalog_health and show me products with
```
