# Partner Program

Every Respira account has access to the partner program at [dashboard/partner](https://www.respira.press/dashboard/partner). Refer a customer, earn lifetime commissions on every dollar they spend with Respira. The program runs in-house — no third-party affiliate platform — which means lower friction, faster payouts, and no shared cookie pool.

## Commission ladder

Commissions ladder up as your lifetime referral revenue grows:

| Lifetime referral revenue | Commission rate |
| --- | --- |
| $0 – $999 | 10% |
| $1,000 – $4,999 | 15% |
| $5,000 – $19,999 | 30% |
| $20,000+ | 50% |

Tier upgrades are automatic and applied retroactively to the start of the current month. Once you hit a tier, you stay at it for as long as your referrals remain active customers.

**Lifetime** means: as long as the referred customer keeps an active Respira subscription, you keep earning. Includes plan upgrades, stacked plans, add-on purchases (e.g., the WooCommerce add-on), and Founder one-time payments.

## Your affiliate link

Every account gets a default affiliate link of the form:

```
https://www.respira.press/?ref=<your-slug>
```

The slug is auto-generated from your account name on first visit to the partner page. You can change it once per month from the *Change slug* card.

The link is a tracked deep-link — it works on any Respira public page, not just the homepage. Examples:

```
https://www.respira.press/pricing?ref=<your-slug>
https://www.respira.press/cli?ref=<your-slug>
https://www.respira.press/blog/april-2026-update?ref=<your-slug>
```

Append `?ref=<your-slug>` (or `&ref=<your-slug>` if there are already query params) to any URL on the public site. The referral cookie persists for 60 days; any signup or purchase from that browser within 60 days is attributed to you.

## What gets attributed

The cookie attaches the `ref` slug at first visit. Attribution events:

- New signup at [respira.press/signup](https://www.respira.press/signup) (or any signup form on the site)
- First paid plan purchase from a Lite/trial account
- Plan upgrade (Maker → Builder, etc.) within the 60-day window
- Plan stack (buying a second Maker on top of an existing one)
- Add-on purchases (WooCommerce add-on)
- Founder plan purchases (one-time, but high-value)

What does **not** count:

- Self-referrals (you can't refer yourself)
- Refunded purchases (commission reverses if the customer refunds within 30 days)
- Free Lite signups (no revenue, no commission — but they count toward your lifetime referral count for tier display purposes)

## Tracking dashboard

The [dashboard/partner](https://www.respira.press/dashboard/partner) page shows:

- **This month's earnings** — pending commissions for the current calendar month
- **Lifetime earnings** — all-time total
- **Referrals** — list of every customer who signed up with your link, what plan they're on, when they started, and how much you've earned from them
- **Clicks** — last 30 days of clicks on your affiliate link, broken out by source URL when available
- **Payout history** — every payout you've received, with date and method

You can export the full referral history as CSV from the *Export* button.

## Payouts

Payouts run on the **15th of each month** for the previous month's earnings, with a $50 minimum. If your earnings for a month are below $50, the balance rolls forward.

Two payout methods supported today:

- **PayPal** (any country PayPal supports). Set your PayPal email in the *Payout settings* card.
- **Wise** (formerly TransferWise). For accounts where PayPal is unavailable or expensive. Set your Wise account from the same card.

Wire transfers are available on request for monthly payouts above $5,000. Email [word@respira.press](mailto:word@respira.press) with your bank details to set up.

## Slug change limits

You can change your affiliate slug **once per month**. The old slug 301-redirects to the new one for 90 days, so existing inbound links keep working during the transition.

If you've shared your link in a podcast, YouTube video, or printed material and need a clean slug forever, that's the move — but think it through, because you can't change it again for 30 days.

## Disclosure

Wherever you share your affiliate link, you should disclose the relationship per FTC, ASA, or your local equivalent. A simple line works:

> *I earn a commission if you sign up for Respira through this link.*

This is a courtesy to your audience and a legal requirement in most jurisdictions.

## Questions

- *Can I refer my own client?* — Yes. As long as you're not paying for their plan, it counts.
- *Do refunds reverse my commission?* — Yes, within 30 days of the original purchase. After 30 days, the commission is locked in.
- *Can I run paid ads pointing at my affiliate link?* — Yes, but check Respira's [acceptable use policy](https://www.respira.press/legal/acceptable-use) — bidding on the term "Respira" in Google Ads is not allowed.
- *Is there a partner directory?* — Not yet. If you'd like to be listed, email [word@respira.press](mailto:word@respira.press).

Open the [partner page](https://www.respira.press/dashboard/partner) to opt in and grab your link.
